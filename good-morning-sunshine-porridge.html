Good Morning Sunshine Porridge

Want a fresh and healthy start in the day without a fuss? Enter porridge.

Don't want to grab overpriced sandwiches on your way to work? Want something healthy? Meet the breakfast allrounder, prepared in as much as five minutes, and carry you through all the way to lunch!

Ingredients

  • 200ml Milk
  • 60g fine Oat flakes or a porridge mix of your choice
  • fresh or dried fruit, berries and nuts
  • optional: yoghurt

Steps

  1. Heat the milk in a pot, avoid it boiling over (unless you have more than the above mentioned five minutes)
  2. Add the oat flakes or porridge mix of your choice, stir with a wire whip for a minute, then switch off the stove
  3. Meanwhile, prepare some fresh fruit, berries or some nuts
  4. Let it rest for a minute and stir again, then put it into your favorite breakfast bowl
  5. Garnish with prepared sides, add some yoghurt if your fancy today and enjoy your Power Breakfast!
