Banana Bread (without sugar!)

Fresh out of the oven Banana Bread with walnuts

This Banana Bread is the ideal companion on a hike, biketour, climbing trip. You can have it as breakfast, lunch, dinner or bedtime candy, if you can withstand to munch it straight out the oven... 😉

As bananas are rich in fructose, this recipe gets along without any additional sugar. Feel free to add any nuts or berries of your choice.
